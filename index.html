✈️ SkyHunt

Hyderabad Airport Treasure Hunt

Collect 10 hidden items. Win exclusive coupons.

⚠️ Important

If your flight departs within the next 45 minutes, please do not play this game. Head to your gate immediately.

🎮 How to Play

  • 🗺️ Open the airport map to see item locations
  • 📷 Walk to that area & open camera
  • ✨ Tap the digital item to collect it
  • 🎁 Collect 5 items → 2 coupons
  • 🏆 Collect 10 items → 4 coupons
  • ⏳ No time limit. Have fun!
